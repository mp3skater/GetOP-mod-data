Frequently Asked Questions

 Why did you make this mod?

We created this mod, to begin with, as a way to implement some cool textured weapons that then caused some crazy chain reaction that made us wanna implement more and more stuff as we went along. This project is my first ever coding project and the longest I have ever persued (1+ years).

 Why should I use this mod?

This mod is for anyone that wants a real challenge in his singleplayer-survival or SMP world, as it includes structures with really good loot and a FINAL dimension of sorts to mark the start and potential (still voluntary) finaly to your world: The person that gets the Herobrine swort first is the King of that world.

 Is it coming for *insert Minecraft version here*?

We'll try our best to port it to later versions, but if you are willing to do so, feel free! If you are done, open up a pull request and We'll get to work on it.

 May I use this in my modpack?

Sure thing! Give credit where it's due.
