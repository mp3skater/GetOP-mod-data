Blocks and Items

This is a collection of all items and blocks that where added with this mod. To see the name of the block/item hover over the different pictures.

Blocks

Painite variants

Painite is an ore that can be found as Painite Ore on the bottom of end islands. An easy way to farm them is by flying with fire rockets and elytra under the islands, place water nearby and mine around as it can also be found in veins. The Painite block is crafted with 9 Painite ingots and gives you the wither effect if you step on it.

Amethyst building blocks

This mod also includes two new building blocks: Amethyst stairs and Amethyst slabs. You can craft them and you will find them in the Castle of Herobrine.

Items

Broken Weapons

These items can be found in all the new structures on the altar in the middle. They can be forged into the non-broken version using the Anvil of Sage and a Painite Ingot.

GetOP Weapons

These are the repaired version of the Broken Weapons. They hold incredible power and all have very strong abilities. For more details see the Weapons page.
