Blocks and Items

So what does this mod contain? Not much, but not too little. Here is the list of the blocks and items that are new in EarlyGame. TapHover on the block/item to reveal its name. All controls in this section use the default controls.

Blocks

Cobblestone Variants

These include but not limited to andesite, granite and diorite. They basically serve the same purpose as a regular cobblestone. You can craft them using their respective loose rocks.

Items

Rocks

These are found lying around the world. You can collect them by either breaking it or interacting with it. These rocks can also be obtained by mining a stone variant, and can be used to craft cobblestone.

Plant Fiber and Lashing

Plant fiber can be obtained by cutting grass and fern using a knife. Combine four of them to make a lashing, which is important for crafting makeshift tools.

Flint Shards

These are obtained by knapping flint--that is, clicking RMB on top of any stone material while holding flint. You can also make flint shards by pressing Shift + RMB with a knife on a small block of rock. These can be used to craft flint tools.

Copper Nuggets

Tools and Weapons

Flint Tier

These are makeshift tools that you make using sticks, flint shards, and some lashing. These guys have very little durability, but it is enough to provide you a decent start for your early game progression, so use them while you still can.

Primitive Tools

Primitive tools are those that are made of pure sticks and lashings. The fire starter behaves like a flint and steel, but there's a random chance that the fire will actually start. Using a slingshot is the same as using a bow, but you use any type of rock as ammo.

Copper Tier

Copper tools are tiered exactly with stone tools and thus cannot be used to mine diamonds, of course. Copper was introduced officially in a 1.17 snapshot, but as of this writing, there are officialy no tools and weapons present for the copper variant yet.

Copper armor is set to be stronger than golden armor but weaker than iron armor. Just the right balance for the early game.

Vanilla Tiers

Knives and saws are new tools added for vanilla materials as well. They basically have the same crafting recipes. Knives are weaker versions of swords, but they have a faster swing cooldown. Additionally, it can be used for breaking grass to collect plant fiber used for crafting lashing. Saws are alternatives to axes, but using them for chopping wood will surely yield you the maximum amount of drop.
